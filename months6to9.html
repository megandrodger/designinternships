Months 6 to 9

By this stage, you should be thinking about the next steps in your career. We want you to be in a position where you can apply for a promotion before the end of the year with us.

Creating a great user experience means working with different design specialisms. You’ll have already spent a lot of time with user researchers and learnt how to identify user needs. Now you'll learn about the other design professions and how you can collaborate to meet user needs.

Training at this stage

  • service design masterclass
  • content design masterclass
  • a day of graphic design with GDS

You’ll have learnt

  • more about the end to end view of services
  • about how we design content
  • how we use things like colour and typography
