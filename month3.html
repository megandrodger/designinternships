Month 3

Prototypes are usually the best thing to put in front of users to test new ideas. They balance the need to be make something quickly, whilst giving users a realistic experience.

There is a steep learning curve for some elements of prototyping - using the command line for the first time can be intimidating. And, there is also the small task of getting your prototypes online (deployed).

You will study front-end development using online courses and practice prototyping on your projects. We will also team you up with an experienced developer, who can mentor you through the world of development.
