Section 4

Communication is an often overlooked part of design. Being able to communicate clearly and confidently with developers and team members is crucial. At times you will also need to explain, and sometimes defend decisions to stakeholders.

Designers need to use qualitative and quantitative research to find solutions to problems. Designing with data is integral to meeting user needs. Google Analytics is used widely across government, and has some great free training. From the three to six month period there will be opportunities to train in these areas, and focus on them in your work.

Training at this stage

  • Google Analytics
  • leadership course
  • public speaking course
  • introduction to digital and agile (24 Apr – 5 May)

You’ll have learnt

  • about quantitative analysis and Google Analytics
  • how to communicate with your team more effectively
  • how to communicate your design decisions to a large group

Milestones after 6 months

After 6 months, you'll have:

  • published 2 to 3 posts on the Home Office Digital blog
