IxD Internship

12 month learning plan

At the Home Office, we design services to meet user needs.

Your jobs as an interaction designer will be to design services so good, people prefer to use the digital version.

You’ll work on important projects like helping to keep the UK’s streets safe and borders secure.

This plan outlines what to expect over the next 12 months – a lot of learning, a lot of doing and a lot of hard work. It’s flexible, so we’ll tailor it to better meet your needs as a designer.
