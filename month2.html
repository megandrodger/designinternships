Month 2

In the second month you’ll settle into a rhythm, working three days a week on a project team with a senior designer. You’ll spend the other two days leading your own project.

A big part of a designer’s job is to champion the user and in most Government services the users are everyone. This means designing accessible services that can be used by the broadest range of people needs to be part of each project's culture from the beginning.

You’ll also start to become more familiar with agile and how designers work in agile teams.

Training at this stage

  • access needs training for designers
  • agile training
  • Jira and the design process

You’ll have learnt

  • how the service standard relates to your projects
  • about access needs, and the types of needs people have
  • how to design accessible services
  • about user research
  • how agile projects function
